HTML provides six levels of headings, from <h1> to <h6>. The <h1> tag represents the most important heading, while the <h6> tag represents the least important heading. Headings are used to structure content and improve readability. They also play a crucial role in SEO (Search Engine Optimization) as search engines use them to understand the hierarchy and relevance of content on a webpage.
